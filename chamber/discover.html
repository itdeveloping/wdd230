Today our Vice President of Training, Lic. Alejandro Robledo Beltrán was as a jury at the “Tamaulipas Exporta” event which was organized by the Faculty of Law and Social Sciences of the Autonomous University of Tamaulipas.

Octuber 27, 2023

The meeting of businesswomen was held, where Ms. Gloria Gallardo Cavazos, Vice President of Businesswomen, gave the welcome words, thanking each of the attendees for their punctual arrival at the meeting. On this occasion, the topic “Breast Cancer Prevention” was discussed with special guest Dr. Ayda Martínez Hernández.

Breast cancer has a cure, it is easy to detect, most cancer detection occurs through routine examinations.

To perform the corresponding exams, consider not wearing substances, such as talcum powder, body creams, perfumes, since due to the chemical content they contain, they can alter the result of the exam.
